# Game Design Document: Serpent's Ascent

## Core Concept

A colorful 3D action-survival evolution game where the player starts as a vulnerable baby snake and evolves through five stages into a mythical five-headed flying Queen Snake.

## Core Loop

Hunt targeted prey, outsmart escalating threats, survive to the required food count, then trigger an instant cinematic evolution into the next snake class.

## Stage 1 Vertical Slice

**Snake:** Baby Snake, neon green.

**Biome:** Suburban Backyard. Grass blades feel huge, garden beds provide cover, the sport court is dangerous open ground, and the stone fire pit provides camouflage.

**Diet:** 10 mice with glowing pink noses.

**Threats:**

- Bluey, a giant curious dog.
- Gardeners with trowels.
- Heavy lawnmower hazards.

**Abilities:**

- Dash under pressure with stamina.
- Stalk to reduce detection.
- Bite to eat nearby mice.
- Dramatic Flop to play dead. Bluey ignores the snake; gardeners flick the snake across the yard as a tactical fast-travel gag.
- Predator Vision to reveal glowing scent trails to prey.

## Future Stages

1. Baby Snake — Backyard, mice, gardeners, lawnmower, Bluey.
2. Rattlesnake — Blooming Desert, rabbits, hikers, pest control, coiled strike, warning rattle, cactus traps.
3. King Cobra — Autumn Forest, deer/moose, ATV hunters, scent tracking, silent stalking, hood flare.
4. Leviathan — Sapphire Ocean, sharks, harpoon boats, submarines, 360-degree swimming, flashbang, constrictor crunch.
5. Queen Snake — Neon Skyline, tanks/helicopters, god-mode flight, five-head attacks, Rainbow Tornado finale.
